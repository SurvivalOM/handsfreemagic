Questions people ask

Is it really free? What's the catch?

Really free, no catch, no locked features. HandsFree Magic is built on proven open-source technology and is brought to you by the Future Proofing Lifestyle network. In future, a small sponsor message inside the app may help keep it free — dictation itself will never be paywalled.

Does my voice or text ever leave my computer?

No. The speech recognition runs entirely on your machine. You can switch off your Wi-Fi and it keeps working — that is the proof.

Windows showed a blue warning when I installed. Why?

That is Windows SmartScreen being cautious with any new, young app it hasn't seen many times yet. Click "More info" and then "Run anyway". The app is open, inspectable, and does not touch the internet during dictation.

Which Windows versions are supported?

Windows 10 and Windows 11, 64-bit. Support for Mac and Linux is on the roadmap.

Which languages can I dictate in?

English is excellent out of the box. In Settings you can download alternative models (like Whisper) that understand dozens of languages including Hindi and Tamil.

Who is behind this?

HandsFree Magic is a Future Proofing Lifestyle tool, built on the excellent open-source project Handy by CJ Pais and contributors (MIT license). We believe good tools should educate, empower, and enlighten — not extract.
